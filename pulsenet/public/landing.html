Features

Everything your hospital needs

From bedside vitals to pharmacy billing — PulseNet connects every touchpoint in your hospital's care workflow.

Real-Time Vitals Monitoring

Nurses record SpO2, temperature, blood pressure, and heart rate every hour. Doctors see live vitals on their patient view.

Digital Prescriptions

Doctors issue structured prescriptions with medicines, dosages, and instructions. Pharmacy receives them instantly for dispensing.

Pharmacy Inventory

Track medicine stock in real-time. Get alerts for low and critical stock levels before they become a patient safety issue.

Lab Test Management

Doctors request tests, lab staff update status, and completed reports are available to the patient and doctor instantly.

Integrated Billing

Automatic charge tracking across room, medicines, tests, and consultations. One consolidated bill for every patient.

Multi-Role Access Control

Six distinct dashboards — each role sees exactly what they need. Admin controls user access, room assignments, and system charges.

How it Works

From admission to discharge — fully connected

PulseNet digitises the complete patient journey. Every action — a vital recorded, a prescription issued, a test completed — flows automatically to the right person.

1

Patient Admission

Admin registers the patient, assigns a room and doctor. The patient gets portal access immediately.

2

Doctor Diagnosis & Prescription

Doctor reviews history, records diagnosis, and issues a digital prescription that reaches pharmacy in real-time.

3

Nurse Care & Monitoring

Nurses log vitals every hour and record medication administration. Doctors see this data live.

4

Discharge & Billing

One consolidated bill is generated — room charges, medicines, tests — reviewed and paid by the patient.
